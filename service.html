What We Offer

From precise land surveys to advanced geospatial analysis, we provide comprehensive solutions for all your surveying and mapping needs

Land Surveying

We offer comprehensive land surveying in accordance with land laws and acts These services include;

  1. Boundary surveys
  2. Subdivisions
  3. Processing of titles
  4. Buying and selling of land
  5. Due diligence on behalf of our clients
  6. Land Successions

Construction Layout

Precise stakeout services for building foundations, utilities, and infrastructure projects. We ensure your construction aligns perfectly with architectural plans and engineering specifications for successful project completion.

Engineering Survey

We provide essential data (using high precision data collection machines) for planning, design, construction and maintenance of engineering works such a Roads, dams, bridges, railway, building etc. This include;

  • Topo data and mapping (topographical survey)
  • Setting out
  • Levelling
  • Deformation monitoring
  • layout designs
  • As-built data
  • Quantity survey (earthworks measurement)
  • hydrographic surveying (if applicable)
  • geodetic control establishment

GIS Services

Comprehensive Geographic Information System solutions including spatial analysis, database development, and custom mapping applications. Transform your spatial data into actionable insights for informed decision-making.
