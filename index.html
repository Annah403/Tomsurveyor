Our Services

We provide comprehensive surveying and geospatial services using advanced technology to deliver precise measurements and detailed analysis for your projects.

Land Surveying

We offer comprehensive land surveying, sectional property surveys, construction layout and staking, GIS services, and high-precision 3D laser scanning.

Engeneering Survey

We provide essential data using high-precision collection equipment for the planning, design, construction, and maintenance of engineering works such as roads, dams, bridges, railways, and buildings.

Topographical and mapping

We provide high-precision 3D laser scanning for buildings, infrastructure, and industrial facilities with detailed point cloud generation.

GIS Mapping

Learn More

Construction Layout

We provide precise construction layout and staking services for buildings, roads, utilities, and infrastructure projects.

Learn More

Property Surveys

In Kenya, sectional property surveys are governed by the Sectional Properties Act and the Survey Act (Cap 299). Our services include preliminary site surveys, building measurements and unit demarcation, preparation and approval of sectional plans, and processing of sectional titles.
